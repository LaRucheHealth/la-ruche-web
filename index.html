Digital Health Solutions

We build progressive digital solutions to facilitate the delivery of care and empower healthcare recipinets to achieve equitable health outcomes

Digitalize

We strengthen the management of health data within medical facilities by delivering an intuitive, web-based Electronic Medical Records solution that empowers health workers to deliver essential ambulatory services with minimal learning effort.

Connect

We increase the health care recipients’ awareness and access to fundamental health services by incentivizing the use of a patient portal for an improved experience every time they come into contact with the healthcare system.

Standardize

We introduce transparency and efficiency through a billing module that helps medical facilities avoid missed revenue as well as track and resolve health insurance claims.

Our Products

Health Worker Portal

platform-independent (progressive web application) EMR solution with offline task execution capabilities, billing and a UI tailored to the unique requirements of end-users.

Patient Portal

A patient portal designed to boost patient engagement and provide patients with access to relevant healthcare content and preventative care services.

Telehealth Portal

Embedded e-consultation features to extend the reach of practitioners to remote communities otherwise cut off from the greater health network.
